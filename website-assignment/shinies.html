Shinies I've Caught

This Pokémon is adorable and I love it, but I can't say I caught this honestly. I obtained this shiny Mew from a cheating device known as an Action Replay, allowing for players to get unlimited items, guaranteed KOs, and instant catches on Pokémon. Sadly I used this Action Replay to hack a shiny Mew to appear and I caught it willy nilly. Like I said it's still cute, and I don't think there is any legal way to catch this Pokémon in game anyway, so I'll keep him around for a while.
This Pokémon I got in the same way that I got Mew, so not much pride goes into catching this one. Latias is very cute with its golden coloring though. I have made it a goal to catch one of these babies legally so that I can have two golden jets and a heart swelled with joy at saying "I found him after 20 hours of soft resetting and I love her."
This Pokémon is actually available to every trainer that play Gold/Silver or HeartGold/SoulSilver. Shiny Gyarados is an event Pokémon that you encounter in the story and it is always shiny. So naturally, everyone is going to have a couple of these. I only have one though that I kept. Maybe I'll keep more so that I can have an army of red dragons to fight with.
I found this shiny Clamperl whilst travelling underwater in my Alpha Sapphire version. Completely random and was not looking for it, much like a lot of other Pokémon on this list. One day I'll figure out what to do with him. He evolves into two different monsters so decisions, decisions...
Shiny Rayquaza was an event Pokémon that was a mystery gift, which is a online service where you can just hook up to the internet and get a cool Pokémon if they have a special holiday or new game release. I have another shiny Rayquaza that was a hacked one, but I like this one better, even though it was just given to me. Black looks so good on this dragon.
This Pokémon was also not being looked for when stumbled upon, and is easily my favorite encounter. I was playing my Alpha Sapphire game for the first time, and I went into the route thinking to myself, "Hmm, I really should get a flying type Pokémon for my team," and the first Pokémon to show up is this shiny Taillow(the pre-evolution of Swellow). I was so ecstatic about finding this bird, I caught it and kept it on my team for a while, loving throwing him out and hearing the sparkles. It was my first shiny that I was able to have on my team because it was early enough in the game. I love love love this green dude.
This Pokémon was another surprise. I found him in my Ultra Sun game. I was actually training my Pokémon team up by killing Gastly's specifically. Then I see this weird colored dude flashing his sparkles at me and to be honest, I screeched.
Here is my Gastly evolved into Haunter! I love the blue tongue.
I was not as excited about this Pokémon because it happened in the same game the day after I found Gastly. So when I saw him pop up and sparkling, I just stared at it for a second, kind of not believing it. Nonetheless, the red brush looks quite nice on Smeargle.
I got this shiny Gardevoir in a WonderTrade, which is where you send off a Pokémon into the internet and get back a random Pokémon sent off by another random guy in the internet. Usually you get a lot of Zubats, but I got this lady, level 100, fully trained and with Pokérus, a rare condition which ups a Pokémon's stats and is contagious to other Pokémon, which is actually a good bug. This is my most recent shiny in my Alpha Sapphire.
