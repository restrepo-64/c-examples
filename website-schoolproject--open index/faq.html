Frequently Asked Questions

Do I Need Anything Special to Start Shiny Hunting?

No, you do not! You can start running in the grass with the intent of finding a shiny right now. All you need is a Pokémon game and some determination.

How Long Does It Take to Find a Shiny Pokémon?

It depends on what method you use. It could take as little as a couple minutes if you're lucky, or you could go months like I currently am trying to soft reset for a shiny starter in my SoulSilver game. Patience is the main aspect here.

In Your Opinion, What Are the Worst Shinies?

There are a lot of shinies that have barely any difference from the originals. Those are the worst. An example being shiny Garchomp. There are also a lot of shinies that for some reason turned into a sickly green color. Tauros is a notable one. I don't know why they thought these were good ideas to let slide after the algorithm spit them out.

So What's So Great About Shiny Hunting? It Seems Like Too Much Work To Be Worth It..

I think it's mostly about having the odds really slim or even against you, and you get them despite that. It's true it is a lot of time and effort to try and get it, but it is really exhilerating to be just droning on after a couple hours fishing up Magikarp and then out of nowhere you hear twinkling and then you see this shiny golden one and it is just really exciting. Just watch this guy on YouTube. He shiny hunts on his Twitch stream and he spends a lot of time searching for his monsters but his reactions are priceless and genuine. (Warning: contains language.)
